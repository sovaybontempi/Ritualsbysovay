Services & Offerings

Spellwork, spirit support, tarot, and custom ritual — brought to you in Virginia Beach, or wherever the working needs to happen.

Rates & process A 15-minute consult is always free — reach out by email and we'll get something on the calendar. Beyond that, the rate is a flat $150/hour plus any travel expenses, current as of July 2026 and subject to change. Sovay works in person whenever possible — she finds the work is simply stronger that way — and brings all materials to you. Online sessions are available if in-person isn't an option. If you're not satisfied with the work, you can request a full refund at any time.

Spells & Charms

Free 15-min online consult

Magickal solutions for mundane problems. Love and relationships, prosperity and abundance, protection, career and goal work, difficult coworkers or neighbors, cleansing and clearing, luck — if it's a light or positive working, it's within scope. Sovay is happy to help you name the problem and find the right working for it, even if you're not sure where to start.

Most of these begin with the free online consult, then move into a one-hour, in-person session where Sovay brings everything needed and the two of you do the working together. Depending on the spell, this often ends with a charm, potion, or herbal working you take home with you. The hourly rate will include all materials unless otherwise noted.

Hexes, Curses & Banishment

30-minute paid consult, then $150/hr + travel if accepted

Most of the time, a positive path forward exists and it's the better one. But not always — sometimes a situation calls for removing someone from it, or protecting yourself from active harm, and Sovay understands that need.

This is the one offering that doesn't start with a free consult. The 30-minute consult is $50 and covers an in depth discussion of the working you're looking to do, the history behind it, and what you're hoping to get out of it. Sovay will often suggest alternative solutions to traditional hexes/curses that minimize harm while still acheiving the desired outcome. If both parties agree to move forward, details on additional cost will be finalized during the consultation before based on amount of time needed to complete the working at Sovay's typical hourly rate.
