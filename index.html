About Sovay

Modern Witch and Spiritual Practitioner

Based in Virginia Beach, VA

A lifelong spiritual seeker, Sovay has an enduring love for shiny rocks, tiny jars, handmade wooden spoons, black teapots and little cauldrons. She enjoys hugging trees, making friends with beetles, buying more books than she will ever read, and lighting little pieces of paper on fire.

Sovay has been a practicing witch since 2018 with a wide range of interests in the pagan arts — tarot, spellcraft, candle magick, moon magick, breathwork, herbology, lucid dreaming, shamanic journeying, psychopomp, spirit and ancestral guide work. She specializes in leading meaningful rituals and creating impactful magickal workings for her local community, and has found deep healing in herself and others through her spiritual practice.

She believes in the power of movement and magic to heal the body and soul, and is grateful to the pagan and metaphysical community for providing a space where everyone is welcome to come exactly as they are. Whether you're seeking spellwork, tarot readings or spiritual guidance, Sovay would love to connect — reach out and say hello.
